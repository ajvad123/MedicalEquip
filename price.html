Brief History

Al Watad Medical is one of the leading companies in the field of medical gases. Established in 1986, the company has over 18 years of expertise in providing state-of-the-art medical systems to renowned hospitals across the Kingdom. Our experience ensures the capability to deliver and install modern, cutting-edge medical gas systems.

Our Scope of Work

  • Design and implement medical gas systems following international standards such as HTM 2022 and DIN.
  • Install and fabricate medical gas piping networks for medical air plants, vacuum plants, and oxygen manifolds.
  • Maintain completed projects, ensuring optimal functionality and preventing malfunctions or stoppages.
  • Conduct on-the-job training programs for medical gas personnel.
  • Participate in health, medical, and science fairs and exhibitions across the Gulf region and the Kingdom.

Our Technical Staff

Our technical team, including engineers and site staff, are highly trained and experienced, proficient in all project stages from design to implementation. Regular training programs in Germany and the UK ensure their expertise aligns with HTM 2022 and German DIN standards. Our pipe welders are certified and accredited by various hospitals for their adherence to international procedures and guidelines.
